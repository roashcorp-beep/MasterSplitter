# Master Splitter — Promotional Video Script (45–60s)

**Goal:** make Master Splitter feel like a premium, App-Store-ready product in under a
minute. Emotional hook → product depth → payoff. Hebrew VO with English-subtitle
variant noted.

**Format:** 1080×1920 (9:16) vertical for social + a 16:9 crop for App Store preview.
**Demo source:** the seeded **Albania 2026** group (see `seed_demo_data.py`).
**Total runtime:** 52s.

**Music:** upbeat, warm electronic-pop, ~110–120 BPM, light pluck synth + soft
four-on-the-floor (think "indie fintech"). One clean beat-drop at the settle moment
(0:34). Suggested: Epidemic Sound "Sunny Days / Feel Good" category, or Artlist
"warm upbeat tech". Duck music −8dB under VO.

**Color/feel:** dark glassmorphism, purple (#a855f7) accent, subtle starfield motion.

## Editing notes
- **Pace:** cuts every 1.5–3s in the first half, slow down at the settle payoff.
- **Transitions:** prefer in-app *crossfades* and *parallax* over hard cuts to feel
  native; never use cheesy wipes.
- **Numbers:** always show real seeded values (₪, €, $, ¥, ALL) — never 0 or Lorem.
- **Hands:** use a soft device mock with a finger cursor; movements eased (no robotic
  linear motion).
- **Captions:** burn-in bold captions (accessibility + sound-off autoplay).
- **CTA end-card:** App Store + Google Play badges + QR to the PWA.

## Hook variants (A/B for ads)
1. "החופשה נגמרה. החובות לא." → *"The trip ended. The debts didn't."*
2. "מפסיקים להיות הקמצן של הקבוצה." → *"Stop being the group's accountant."*
3. "5 חברים. 4 מטבעות. חשבון אחד נקי." → *"5 friends. 4 currencies. One clean bill."*
